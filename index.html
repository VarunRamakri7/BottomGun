How to play

  • Drag up or down (mouse or finger) to control flight height.
  • Drag left or right to change camera zoom (field of view) and forward speed.
  • Collect cyan pickups for energy.
  • Avoid red obstacles — they drain energy and knock you back.
  • Esc pauses. Sound starts muted — use Enable sound if you want audio.
